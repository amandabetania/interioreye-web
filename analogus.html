Triadic

A triadic color scheme is a type of color scheme used in design that involves three colors equidistant from each other on the color wheel. This arrangement creates a balanced and harmonious look while offering more color variety than a monochromatic or analogous scheme.

Example

Design

Triadic color schemes are versatile and can be used in various design contexts, from interior design and fashion to graphic design and art. They are especially suitable for creating a lively and dynamic visual impact while maintaining a sense of cohesion in the color palette.

Color Pallete

When using a triadic color scheme, it's essential to consider the proportions of each color to maintain balance in the overall design. Typically, one color is used as the dominant hue, with the other two serving as accents. This helps prevent the color scheme from becoming overwhelming or chaotic.
